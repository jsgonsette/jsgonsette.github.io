microCeption

This FREE interactive tool is designed to demystify the inner workings of a computer's central processing unit (CPU) by allowing you to explore a simplified model of a CPU down to its fundamental components. Inspired by educational resources developed by J.D. Carpinelli, our simulator is perfect for students and enthusiasts eager to learn about computer architecture and CPU operation.

What you can do with microCeption

Visualize CPU Components See real-time representations of CPU registers like the Program Counter (PC), Accumulator (AC), and others as they operate.

Run Instructions Load and execute simple assembly-like instructions to see how they affect different parts of the CPU.

Understand CPU Sequencing Observe how instructions are fetched, decoded, and executed in a step-by-step process.

Experiment Freely Create your own sequences of operations to see how changes affect outputs.
